Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-16 · Applies to the Flock Dodger web app and Android wrapper.

Summary

Flock Dodger is a privacy-focused routing helper. We do not require an account, do not run ads, and do not sell your data. Most information stays on your device.

Data we process

  • Addresses you type — sent to the public OpenStreetMap Nominatim geocoding service to convert text to coordinates.
  • Start/end coordinates — sent to the public OSRM routing service to compute driving routes.
  • Map area — sent to OpenStreetMap Overpass servers to load community-mapped cameras in the visible region.
  • Location (optional) — only if you tap “use my location” or enable Trip mode; used on-device to set start or show cameras ahead. Not uploaded to a Flock Dodger server (we don’t operate one for analytics).
  • On-device storage — saved routes, camera reports, quality votes, saved areas, and settings use browser / app local storage only.

Third-party services

Map tiles, geocoding, routing, and camera queries use third-party public APIs (for example OSM/Nominatim, OSRM, Overpass, CARTO tiles). Those operators may see IP addresses and request content under their own policies. We do not control their logs.

What we do not do

  • No accounts or profiles
  • No advertising SDKs or tracking pixels for monetization
  • No sale of location or route history
  • No proprietary Flock Safety account integration

Payments

Optional support may open third-party payment apps (for example Cash App) that you choose to use. Payment providers process those transactions under their own terms. Flock Dodger does not receive your bank credentials.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. Do not use it to collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as features change. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do.

Contact

For privacy questions about this project, open an issue on the public GitHub repository for Flock Dodger or contact the maintainer listed there.
